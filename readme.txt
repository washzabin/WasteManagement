Clicking on the cross button in the top-right corner will exit the program prematurely.

Run the program.
Click on the button that says Click to Donate.
In the next page, select any one of the options among Other, Perishable, or Non-Perishable.
Selecting these options will generate a list of generic items.
Specific items can be documented in the Comments section too.
Clicking on Submit will take you to the Contact Information page.
Please enter all fields marked with an asterisk (*) and click on the button that says Validate.
Once all inputs are validated, please click on Submit & Exit.
Please check the email account you entered for an email from thewastemanagementteam@gmail.com.
The email will contain information on when and where to keep your donation items.
